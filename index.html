About Me

James Smith, British entrepreneur and developer based in San Francisco.

I'm the co-founder of Bugsnag with Simon Maynard, and from 2009 to 2012 I led up the product team as CTO of heyzap.com.

In the past I've built a few other web and facebook apps (wegot.tv, helpthirst.com, sponsor-me facebook app), and I used to work as a forex financial developer at Bloomberg's European headquarters in London.
